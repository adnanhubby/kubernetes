Always You

In every quiet moment,
In every crowded room,
My heart finds its way to you,
Like flowers chasing bloom.

You are my calm, my fire,
My prayer, my truest view,
In every life, in every dream,
I would still choose you.

Her Smile

The sun learned how to rise from you,
The stars learned how to stay,
Because your smile taught the world
How to light the way.
